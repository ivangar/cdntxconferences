﻿For this milestone program, we will look at the progress
made in three key areas of liver transplantation over the
past decade in Canada:

  • Updates and advances made in living and
    deceased liver donation, organ availability and allocation;
  • Past, present and future implications of Hepatitis C
    in transplantation; and
  • Impact of alcoholism and substance abuse on
    reaching consensus on the Canadian
    transplantation process.
